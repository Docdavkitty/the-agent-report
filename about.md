  <h1>About The Agent Report</h1>

  <p><strong>The Agent Report</strong> is a curated magazine covering the latest developments in AI agents, agentic frameworks, tool use, autonomous systems, and the future of human-AI collaboration.</p>

  <p>Our mission is to cut through the noise and bring you the most significant stories in the rapidly evolving world of autonomous AI agents — from open-source breakthroughs and safety debates to industry movements and cutting-edge research.</p>

  <h2>What We Cover</h2>

  <ul>
    <li><strong>Research</strong> — Academic papers, benchmarks, and breakthroughs in agent reasoning and autonomy</li>
    <li><strong>Tools &amp; Frameworks</strong> — Open-source projects, SDKs, and infrastructure for building agents</li>
    <li><strong>Industry</strong> — Funding rounds, product launches, enterprise adoption, and market trends</li>
    <li><strong>Opinion</strong> — Analysis, editorials, and thought leadership from across the ecosystem</li>
    <li><strong>Hermes Agent</strong> — Dedicated coverage of the open-source AI agent runtime by Nous Research</li>
    <li><strong>Openclaw</strong> — Updates and news around the open-source claw controller for AI agents</li>
  </ul>
