  <h1>About The Agent Report</h1>

  <p><strong>The Agent Report</strong> is a curated magazine covering the rapidly evolving world of <strong>AI agents</strong> — autonomous systems, agentic frameworks, tool use, multi-agent orchestration, and the shifting frontier of human-AI collaboration.</p>

  <p>We track developments across research labs, open-source communities, startups, and big tech. Our goal is to help engineers, researchers, and builders stay informed about what matters in the agentic AI space.</p>

  <h2>Sections</h2>

  <p><strong>🔬 Research</strong> — Papers, benchmarks, and scientific breakthroughs in agent architectures, reasoning, planning, memory, and tool use.</p>

  <p><strong>🛠️ Tools & Frameworks</strong> — Open-source frameworks, SDKs, and platforms for building agents: LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGPT, OpenAI Agents SDK, Anthropic's MCP, and more.</p>

  <p><strong>🏢 Industry</strong> — Product launches, funding rounds, enterprise adoption, and market trends in the AI agent ecosystem.</p>

  <p><strong>💭 Opinion</strong> — Analysis, predictions, and perspectives on where agentic AI is heading and what it means for builders.</p>
